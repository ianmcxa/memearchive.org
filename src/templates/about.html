About the Meme Archive

The Memearchive is a site dedicated to preserving memes in a transcribed and searchable format. Memes are an important part of internet culture, and they are easily lost over time as sites go down or remove old content. Many memes contain text that is not searchable making them very difficult to find. Memearchive seeks to fix this problem by storing each meme with a transcription of its text. Anyone can submit a meme to the archive, and we hope that you will join us in our efforts to preserve these valuable cultural artifacts.

About the Project

Memearchive was created partially because we were having trouble finding memes from years ago, and partially because the amazing domain, memearchive.org, was available.
